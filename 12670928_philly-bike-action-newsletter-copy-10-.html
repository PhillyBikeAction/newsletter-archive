January 2024 Newsletter

Philly Bike Action is a group devoted to improving bike infrastructure for all of Philadelphia. 
News
It has been a fantastic year for Philly Bike Action! What started as a group of friends wanting better infrastructure has grown into one of the lead organization in Philadelphia fighting for safer streets. We have come a long way this year and are stoked to see what 2024 brings! Below are the upcoming events for the month of January. 
 
Upcoming Events
Monday January 1st:
  • Join us for the first social bike ride of 2024! We will be meeting at 8am at the south face of City Hall before the parade! We will ride until the parade starts.

Wednesday January 3rd:
  • Repaving Party Planning Meeting: Time to start planning the Repaving Party (i.e. PBA's 1 year anniversary party). We need a few more people to join the organizing committee, come for a drink!
Monday January 8th:
  • January 2024 Citywide Meeting: This month we welcome a panel of bike-friendly RCO board members who will talk about why getting involved in your neighbor association matters, how to do it, and answer our questions about how best to engage with RCOs for safe street advocacy.
Monday January 15th:
  • Philly Bike Action District 2 Meeting: Calling all residents of District 2 (map - south of Market, west of Broad) and folks looking to contribute to our efforts to get a new bike lane installed in Center City from Market to South Street.
Monday January 22nd: 
  • Philly Bike Action District 1 Meeting: The District 1 monthly meeting will be on Monday January 22nd at 7:00pm at Piazza Alta in Northern Liberties. Address is:
  • Piazza Alta
  • 1099 Germantown Ave
    Philadelphia, PA 19123
Volunteer Opportunities
There are many ways to get involved with Philly Bike Action. These are the roles we are currently trying to fill. Reply to this email or reach out on our Discord server if you are interested:
  • District Leaders: We are looking for leaders to host monthly meetups and events in their own districts. We are looking for district leaders for all City Council District from 1-10 right now. The goal is to share this responsibility between 2 other people in the same district. 
  • Bike lane design team: We need designs for new bike lanes. You can design one in your own neighborhood or wherever you like. Reach out and we will teach you all there is to know. When it is done, it will be published HERE on our website. We even have a guide so you can do it yourself. 
PBA Stickers
Philly Bike Action currently has stickers available. We recommend a donation of $10 for every 100. We will do deliveries within the city Philadelphia. Reply to this email or reach out on our Discord server if you would like some. We will also have some available at our monthly Citywide meeting.

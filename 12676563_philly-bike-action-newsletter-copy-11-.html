February 2024 Newsletter

Philly Bike Action is a group devoted to improving bike infrastructure for all of Philadelphia. 
News
February is here and we are excited to share the latest developments of the past month!

Nonprofit status: Philly Bike Action will be incorporating as a 501c4 nonprofit! This means we will be able to start collecting donations to fund our regular meetings and events. As a 501c4 we will be able to engage in political speech (including candidate endorsement) however donations are not tax deductible. We will send out a special email whenever this process is complete.

District Meetings: We will be having district meetings in our 5 most active City Council districts (districts 1 to 5) this month! This is a huge deal! Involvement in local politics is essential in making our streets safer for biking. We are happy to be hosting meetings in half of the Philly's districts on a regular basis now. Please reply to this email if you need help setting up meetings in Districts 6-10.

Volunteer Opportunities
There are many ways to get involved with Philly Bike Action. These are the roles we are currently trying to fill. Reply to this email or reach out on our Discord server if you are interested:

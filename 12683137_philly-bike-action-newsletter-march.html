March 2024 Newsletter

Philly Bike Action is a group devoted to improving bike infrastructure for all of Philadelphia. 
Big news!

Philly Bike Action is now able to accept donations!

Your donation will help us cover recurring costs of meeting room rentals and tech services, printing costs, and the materials and supplies we use in our Direct Action events.

If you'd like to support our mission, click here to set up a monthly donation or make a one-time contribution. 

Philly Bike Action is a social welfare organization incorporated in the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania. Contributions to Philly Bike Action are not deductible as charitable contributions for federal income tax purposes.
News
Bike Lane Parties! We will be starting Bike Lane Party demonstrations in April to campaign for the end of bike lane parking permits on Spruce and Pine Streets. If you would like to be involved with the planning for this effort, email Caleb at caleb@bikeaction.org

Nonprofit and Board: Philly Bike Action is incorporating as a 501c4 nonprofit! As part of this process we have established a Board with representatives from five City Council districts. The Board meets monthly, resolutions will be posted publicly on the Discord, our bylaws can be found on the website, and elections will be held in 2026.

Tech Committee The Board has chartered an official Tech Committee to oversee the technical needs of Philly Bike Action. Keep an eye on our events page for ways to get involved, both as a contributor or with ideas about how to improve the PBA experience!

November 2023 Newsletter

Philly Bike Action is a group devoted to improving bike infrastructure for all of Philadelphia. 
News
Sunday Slow Ride: Thanks to everyone who attended the Sunday Slow Ride this year. We will be taking a break and recalibrating for the winter. However, we plan to continue the ride with renewed vigor in the springtime. We will also remain busy connecting with congregations, city council, and local RCOs to end the permitted practice of bike lane parking on Spruce and Pine Streets. If you would like to get involved and help out, reach out on our Discord or email us at info@bikeaction.org 

SURVEY: We want your feedback on the Sunday Slow Ride / Church Permit Project: https://forms.gle/fAFXeuvyjpSrgNSGA

Newsletter: We are changing the frequency of our newsletter. From now on, the newsletter will be sent on a monthly basis and will highlight any events for the upcoming month.

Volunteer Opportunities
There are many ways to get involved with Philly Bike Action. These are the roles we are currently trying to fill. Reply to this email or reach out on our Discord server if you are interested:
  • District Leaders: We are looking for leaders to host monthly meetups and events in their own districts. We are looking for district leaders for all City Council District from 1-10 right now. The goal is to share this responsibility between 2 other people in the same district. 
  • Bike lane design team: We need designs for new bike lanes. You can design one in your own neighborhood or wherever you like. Reach out and we will teach you all there is to know. When it is done, it will be published HERE on our website. We even have a guide so you can do it yourself. 
PBA Stickers
Philly Bike Action currently has stickers available. We recommend a donation of $10 for every 100. We will do deliveries within the city Philadelphia. Reply to this email or reach out on our Discord server if you would like some. We will also have some available at our monthly Citywide meeting.

Campaign Update:
Vision Zero Hearing and #FiveSavesLives

Our Vision Zero capital budget demand for $5M is on City Council's lips!



The call for $5 million for Vision Zero funding is picking up steam, with multiple councilmembers expressing their support. Over 30 PBA advocates rallied at City Hall in sub-freezing temps for a hearing investigating the City's progress on reducing traffic deaths.

See our photo recap and watch our bike-powered billboard make its way to City Hall. Watch the hearing online and don't miss PBA's mic drop testimony at 1:46:00.

Sign our petition to let the City know why street safety is important to you!

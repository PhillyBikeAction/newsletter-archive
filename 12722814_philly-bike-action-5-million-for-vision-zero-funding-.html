Mayor Parker: Fund Vision Zero!

It's Budget Season at City Hall! From now through April we are fighting for increased funding to the budget line that saves lives.

Tell Mayor Parker and City Council - it's time for our City to back up its commitment to safety and contribute $5 million to Vision Zero in the Fiscal Year 2026 Capital Budget!

Vision Zero funding pays for projects that are proven to reduce traffic fatalities and severe injuries on our streets like:

  • Improving visibility at intersections
  • Reducing crossing distances for pedestrians
  • Kid-friendly school slow zones
  • Protected bike lanes
  • Road modifications that reduce speeding—the leading cause of deaths.
In 2024, a string of tragic and preventable deaths lead to unprecedented public outcry for safe streets. This year, it's time for Mayor Parker to give Vision Zero the funding it needs to succeed!

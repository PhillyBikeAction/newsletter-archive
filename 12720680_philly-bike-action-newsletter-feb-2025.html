News
  • Calling Fishtown! PBA is holding its first Riverwards Social Meetup. Make friends then get organized! 
     
  • We cleaned over 15 blocks of bike lanes on Walnut and Chestnut Streets. With over 20 volunteers and a visit from Councilmember Jamie Gauthier, PBA cleared debris and snow from this heavily-used bike route in West Philly. See our Instagram post!
     
  • 📝 The Green Lane Bridge rehabilitation project that connects Manayunk and Bala Cynwyd is proposing the removal of sidewalks and adding wider traffic lanes. Help PBA call for a safer design including greater pedestrian and bicycle access in our petition!
     
  • Philly Bike Action dropped some juicy example renders of what concrete protection on Spruce and Pine could look like! If you like these designs, make sure you sign our petition!
     
  • Last Month to Donate to Black Girl Joy Bike Ride! Founder Iresha Picot has teamed up with Philly Bike Action to expand equity in Philadelphia’s bike scene and reach more Black Women. Donate by Feb 28!
     
  • Op-Ed: PBA and Center City Residents Association member, Duncan Adkins, wrote an op-ed for the Philadelphia Inquirer supporting concrete protection on Spruce and Pine Streets. “If Hoboken can achieve zero traffic fatalities, why can’t Philadelphia?”
     
  • A whopping 70 people attended our January 2025 Kickoff meeting despite the snow! Did you miss the meeting? View our presentation.
     
  • Do you live or work near Lehigh Avenue? The City wants to hear from you on their proposed street changes!
     
  • Get involved and stay in the loop on the City's Vision Zero’s 2025 - 2030 Action Plan!

  • Our Tech Team is looking for self-motivated contributors to our GitHub and Django back end! Email tech@bikeaction.org.
     
  • Outreach Coordinator to help organize volunteers and supplies to table for PBA at community events!

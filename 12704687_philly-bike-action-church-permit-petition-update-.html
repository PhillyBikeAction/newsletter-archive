Thank you for signing our petition. Campaign win!


 

Thank you for signing Philly Bike Action's petition to Withdraw the Bike Lane Parking Permits on Spruce and Pine St - we received over 2,000 signatures from Philadelphians like you.

We're happy to announce that this campaign is officially over and has been a huge success:

Yesterday afternoon, Temple Beth Zion-Beth Israel (BZBI) announced that it received approval for a new parking permit that does not include the Spruce Street bike lane. With this decision, there are now no religious institution that hold a permit to park cars in the Spruce and Pine Street bike lanes.

Now, the path to concrete protection of the Spruce and Pine bike lanes is clearer than ever. Keep using your voice to demand action from City leaders: concrete protected bike lanes and increase Vision Zero funding to do it.



Join us on Sept 5 at the Art Museum at 8:30am for a ride to City Hall for a short 9:15am rally. RSVP to give public comment on bike legislation during the 10am City Council meeting.

Event Reminder

Philly Bike Action is a group devoted to improving bike infrastructure for all of Philadelphia. 
Sunday Slow Ride Tomorrow!
Tomorrow (10/8/23) Philly Bike Action will be hosting the last Sunday Slow Ride of 2023. The ride is to bring attention to the bike lane parking permits along Spruce and Pine streets. This is a blatantly unsafe practice that puts cyclists in danger and denies the space for those who need it the most (children, disabled, and the elderly). The permits are requested by the 5 congregations along Spruce and Pine streets and signed by the Philadelphia Streets department. We are asking that these permits no longer be sought or renewed when they expire at the end of the year. 

We will be meeting at the Schuylkill River Park at 9:30am and riding from 10am to 11am. We will be going for lunch afterwards so feel free to join. We look forward to hearing your thoughts on the ride and how it could be improved if the permits are renewed for 2024. 

More information on the ride can be found here: 
https://bikeaction.org/event/sunday-slow-ride-pba-event-2/?swcfpc=1

If you would like to share your concerns with the Philadelphia Streets Department, the Right of Way Unit (unit signing the permits) can be reached at 215-686-5621. You can also reach out the the District Councilmembers Kenyatta Johnson and Mark Squilla here: https://phlcouncil.com/council-members/

Thanks to everyone who has been a part of these rides! We look forward to riding with you in the future.

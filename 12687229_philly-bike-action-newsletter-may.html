News
  • Our Bike Lane Parties were covered in the Inquirer!



    April's Bike Lane Parties were a huge success! We kept the bike lane open while we grooved to tunes and engaged with local residents and congregants at Tenth Presby. The party continues on May 12!
     
  • Our campaign to withdraw the Spruce-Pine bike lane parking permits has over 1400 signatures and we've sent over 500 comments to OTIS, who we believe has a responsibility to find an alternative arrangement.


 
  • Donations raised have fully funded our Citywide, District 1 and 2 meeting venue fees.

    Become a supporter for $5/mo to help cover our digital services and fund meeting space for Districts 3-5.

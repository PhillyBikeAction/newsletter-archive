Glenn Colville killed by driver on Aramingo

On April 12, 2026, Glenn Colville was killed by a driver while riding his bike in the Aramingo Ave bike lane under the railroad tracks near Lehigh, when a driver drove through the plastic flexposts and killed him. He was a father of three young girls.

Concrete protection could have saved Glenn’s life. But the City's plan to bring safety improvements including protected bike lanes to Aramingo has been in bureaucratic limbo since 2024.

Aramingo is a state road, which means PennDOT can fix this. Click the button to send a pre-filled email asking them to:

  • Install “quick-build” safety improvements with hard materials on Aramingo Ave
  • Fund redesigns of Aramingo and Lehigh Ave.

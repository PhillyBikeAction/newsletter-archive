Parker proposes $5M for Vision Zero 
PLUS $5M for Spruce and Pine concrete protection 🎉

Tell City Council to pass this budget!

This is no April Fools joke. Mayor Parker has proposed a significant increase to Vision Zero funding thanks to an unprecedented year of advocacy from the bike community. What is in the proposed budget?

  • $3.5 mil for Vision Zero Capital (this pays for Complete Street projects)
  • $1.5 mil for Traffic Calming (this pays for speed cushions)
  • $5 mil for "concrete barriers" for Spruce and Pine Street 🎉

While not quite hitting our specific ask, the combined budget for "the two Vision Zero line items" (as the administration is now calling them) does equal $5 million and constitutes a x3 increase from her previous budget. 

Let's be clear: $5 million is not enough to end all traffic deaths and continued investment is needed, but this increase represents a major course correction to budgetary priorities that were dangerously off track.

Spruce and Pine: The budget specifically adds an extra $5 mil for concrete barriers! We need to stay on top of Mayor Parker to ensure this isn't an empty promise! CBS.

This budget is not final. City Council needs to pass the budget by June. Contact the Mayor and City Council representatives (including at-large) to keep the pressure on.

June 2: PBA Citywide Meeting with the PPA

Our June Citywide Meeting will feature the Philadelphia Parking Authority! 

Guest Speaker: Richard Lazer (Executive Director) will join us for a discussion about bike lane parking enforcement, daylighting, and new safety and mobility projects like camera-enforcement and crosswalk parking. 

Come for a productive conversation about how safer parking can make for safer streets. There will be pizza!

Monday June 2, 2025 | 6:30 PM – 8:00 PM
Philadelphia Ethical Society (1906 Rittenhouse Square)

News

  • We updated our website! See all the ways you can get involved!
  • Council President Johnson has called for a hearing to look into citywide daylighting! Stay tuned for hearing info. Learn how daylighting works.
  • Report traffic safety concerns with this interactive map tool by May 16. Comments gathered will help DVRPC (Philly's regional planning authority) identify future pedestrian and bicycle safety projects - fill it out!
  • Safer Lehigh Ave? Provide input at the School Slow Zone community event May 8 at Julia De Burgos (401 W Lehigh Ave) from 5-7 PM. 
  • Our Riverwards "district" is ramping up! Next social meetup is May 7.
  • In April, we organized a bike lane clean up of 22nd St with Center City Residents Association. The next one is May 17. Spring Garden on May 3.
  • Last week, PBA celebrated TWO YEARS of successful advocacy! Despite the rain, we had a big turnout, plenty of food, and a great time!
  • South Philly has spoken: they want intersection daylighting and more bike lanes! Check out the votes from our table at Flavors on the Ave.
  • PBA led a walking tour of Old City's new bike lanes on Market and Race while supporting businesses affected temporarily by construction.
  • We brought a big turnout to comment at the Mayor's Budget Town Hall in South Philly, where she mentioned her support for $5m for Vision Zero.
  • CHOP is planning a 1,000 car parking garage in Grays Ferry. The driveway will endanger people in the bike lane. Here's a petition to oppose the project.

Spruce & Pine Pop-Up Protection!

We were out with our hand-made "concrete" curbs to demonstrate how a protected bike lane could look and function on Spruce and Pine streets (Instagram).

Concrete curbs:

  • keep cars from parking illegally in the bike lane
  • prevent cars from passing in or driving down the bike lane like this
  • calm traffic and slow turning vehicle speeds
  • if hit, absorb impact, protecting the bike lane and sidewalk

They are also sized and spaced to:

  • allow people to cross mid-block or get out of a car
  • allow people using the bike lane (incl. wheelchair users) to enter/exit
  • and still allow EMS/fire vehicles to mount if needed

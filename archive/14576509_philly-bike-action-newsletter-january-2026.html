7 bicyclists killed in 2025.

January 2026 Newsletter

Teddy Einstein and June Rodriguez

Two people were killed while riding their bicycles last month. Teddy Einstein, a PBA member and West Philly father of two was killed while biking in Upper Darby Township. On Dec 20, June Rodriguez, father and popular local DJ, was killed in a hit-and-run while biking home from work on Lancaster Ave.

In 2025, a total of 7 people were killed while biking in Philadelphia. This is more than double the number last year and only 3 less than the all-time-high of 10 in 2023. 

Teddy and June were both killed on state-owned roads with unprotected bike lanes. Vision Zero/Complete Street projects save lives where they are installed, but their toolkit is restricted or deprioritized on state roads. Until officials prioritize infrastructure that prevents speeding, we will not see a meaningful reduction in deaths.
